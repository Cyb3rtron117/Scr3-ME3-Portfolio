I am Dian Ehrke

Gameplay Programmer

Hello, I'm Dian Ehrke

I am a Gameplay Programmer based in South Africa, currently pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Game Design.

I have a passion for both playing and creating games, and I am constantly seeking opportunities to expand my knowledge and improve my skills.
I enjoy solving complex problems through code and bringing interactive experiences to life.

My interest in game development began at a young age through creative activities such as building with LEGO. As I grew older, I started experimenting with Minecraft commands and custom creations, which sparked my fascination with programming and game systems.
This passion ultimately led me to pursue a career in game development, where I can transform ideas into engaging and interactive experiences.

In 2025, I completed a Higher Certificate in Game Design and Production at SAE Institute.
Building on that foundation, I am currently continuing my studies toward a Bachelor's Degree in Game Design.
